Iconic Chocolate Brands

🇨🇭 Switzerland
Lindt
Founded 1845

Masters of the conching process, Lindt crafts some of the world's smoothest milk and dark chocolates. Their iconic LINDOR truffles melt into a silky liquid centre.

Truffles Excellence Luxury
✨
🇮🇹 Italy
Ferrero Rocher
Founded 1946

The golden ambassador of Italian confectionery — a whole hazelnut encased in creamy filling, crisp wafer shell, and fine milk chocolate dusted with hazelnut pieces.

Hazelnut Gifting Premium
👑
🇧🇪 Belgium
Godiva
Founded 1926

Born in Brussels, Godiva has epitomised Belgian luxury chocolate for nearly a century. Renowned for their elaborate pralines, ganaches, and seasonal collections.

Pralines Belgian Artisan
🌿
🇫🇷 France
Valrhona
Founded 1922

The choice of three-Michelin-star pastry chefs worldwide. Valrhona sources rare single-origin cacao from Ecuador, Venezuela, and Madagascar for unmatched complexity.

Single-Origin Chef's Choice Dark
⛰️
🇨🇭 Switzerland
Toblerone
Founded 1908

As iconic as the Matterhorn it mirrors, Toblerone blends Swiss milk chocolate with honey and almond nougat into its unmistakable triangular peaks.

Nougat Iconic Shape Travel

🇬🇧 United Kingdom
Cadbury
Founded 1824

Beloved across generations, Cadbury Dairy Milk remains one of the world's best-selling chocolate bars. George Cadbury pioneered ethical cocoa sourcing in the 19th century.

Dairy Milk Heritage Classic

Kinder
Founded 1968

Ferrero's gift to childhood — Kinder Surprise has delighted children for over 50 years with its thin milk chocolate shell, white cream layer, and legendary surprise toys.

A World of Flavour

Chocolate Varieties

🍫
Dark Chocolate

The purist's choice. Rich in flavonoids and complex tannins, dark chocolate offers deep, earthy notes of fruit, spice, and roasted cocoa with minimal sweetness.

50%–100% Cacao
🥛
Milk Chocolate

The world's most beloved variety. Milk solids temper the intensity of cocoa, creating a sweet, creamy, and mellow profile enjoyed by all ages.

25%–45% Cacao
🤍
White Chocolate

Crafted from cocoa butter, milk, and sugar — without cocoa solids. A delicate, buttery sweetness with notes of vanilla and caramel.

Cocoa Butter Only
🌸
Ruby Chocolate

The 21st century's new category, discovered by Barry Callebaut in 2017. Made from naturally pink ruby cacao beans, it has a fruity, berry-like tang.

Naturally Pink
🍯
Caramel Chocolate

Created by slow-heating white chocolate until it caramelises. Blonde and toasty with butterscotch depth — popularised by Valrhona's Dulcey range.

Blonde & Caramelised
🌱
Single-Origin

Chocolate made from cacao harvested from a single plantation or region. Like fine wine, it expresses the unique terroir of its birthplace — earthy, floral, or fruity.

Pure Provenance
The Spectrum

Cacao Intensity Guide

From Sweet to Intensely Pure

The percentage of cacao in a chocolate bar defines its character — from the silky sweetness of milk chocolate to the assertive bitterness of a 100% cacao bar. Understanding this spectrum transforms the way you taste.

Elite chocolatiers like Valrhona and Lindt label their bars by exact cacao percentage, allowing connoisseurs to trace the precise balance between sweetness and complexity.
